In this experiment, there are a total of 100 successive trials. A fixation cross is shown in the center of the screen throughout the experiment. After a random amount of time, you will be shown a red or green square to the left or right of the fixation cross. Your task is to indicate as quickly as you can -- whether the square is red or green. If you make a mistake (i.e., report a green square as red) the trial will be repeated.
